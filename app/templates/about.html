About Google Audit Service

Google Audit Service is a collection of tools that enable Universities and Enterprises that leverage Google Apps, Gmail, and other services to audit security concerns within their domain. Google Audit Service uses Google's API to ingest meta-data about your users, such as login history, to correlate and detect malicious activity. {% endblock %}
